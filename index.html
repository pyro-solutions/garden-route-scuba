Scuba Diving School and Adventure Centre

We offer a wide range of scuba dive courses to meet the needs of our divers.
These courses range from beginner to PADI professional levels, we also offer Open Water courses.
We pride ourselves on exciting dives, super friendly service, state of the art - well maintained equipment and a well stocked shop with affordable diving gear.


About Us

Garden Route Scuba Divers is situated in the Mosselbay Waterfront. We offer Scuba diving, PADI scuba dive training, cylinder fills, snorkeling and gear rentals in and around the Garden Route.

We opened our Mossel Bay shop in 2016 and in 2019 we moved to our current premises at the Mossel Bay Waterfront.

We are the only registered PADI centre in town.


Dive Trips

Our staff will keep you safe, ensure that you are comfortable with the gear and go out of their way to show you the treasures that are to be discovered under the waves of Mossel Bay and the rest of the Garden Route.

Qualified divers, just looking to discover some new dive sites, are also welcome.
Bring your own equipment or buy/rent whatever you need from our on site shop.

See "Our Shop" section for more details.
